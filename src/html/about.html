Welcome to Lexie Card Co.
My name is Lexie and I’m a big fan of snail mail.
My Story.
When I was little, I made cards for my family under the name Lexie Cards. In the fifth grade, it transformed into Lexie Card Co. Now that I’m 23, I wanted to bring my creations to the world. LCC was founded to spread joy through unique, hand-drawn cards. The mission at LCC is to spread joy through vintage communication. It is your new one-stop shop for sending special messages to special people!
Communication
We believe in open, true, and gorgeous communication. This can take on many forms. Here at LCC, our medium of choice is mail. Handwritten cards, notes, and letters have the intimate power of bringing people together regardless of distance. With stamps included, we’re here to make communicating through snail mail as easy as possible!
Community
LCC was built upon the support of our community. It is our mission to continuously support non-profit organizations that are fighting for the necessary changes in our communities throughout this country. A portion of each sale will be donated to a featured non-profit.
Creativity
LCC recognizes the importance of envisioning ideas and taking action. Creativity isn’t just about imagining, it’s also about producing. We’re here to spark your creative genius. That’s why our cards are blank on the inside, so you can fill them with your brilliant and thoughtful words. So pick out your favorite pen and tell someone a story.
